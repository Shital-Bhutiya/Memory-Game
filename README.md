## __*`How to play`*__
1. Click on cards to see the image behind it. The timer will start, By clicking first card.
 
 <kbd><img src="https://github.com/ShitalSB/Memory-Game/blob/master/Readme_Images/click.gif"     width="450px" height="550px"/></kbd>

2. Click on two cards to match the images behind it. Every two click,  moves counter will be increase by 1. Every 8 moves, one star will be removed.

   +  If those clicked cards match with each other, it will turn to green and stay over.
    
    <kbd><img src="https://github.com/ShitalSB/Memory-Game/blob/master/Readme_Images/match.gif" width="450px" height="550px" /></kbd>
    
   +  If they don't match, then it will turn back after few moments.
    
    <kbd><img src="https://github.com/ShitalSB/Memory-Game/blob/master/Readme_Images/unmatch.gif" width="450px" height="550px"  /></kbd>
    
3. Continue selecting cards until all cards match.
4. After matching all cards you will see **Fireworks** and a congratulation popup.
   + You can play it again by pressing `Play Again` button.
  
   <kbd><img src="https://github.com/ShitalSB/Memory-Game/blob/master/Readme_Images/won.gif" width="700px" height="550px"/></kbd>

5. You can reset whole game by pressing the `reset` icon. It will reset the timer, moves, stars and shuffle the game.

  <kbd><img src="https://github.com/ShitalSB/Memory-Game/blob/master/Readme_Images/reset.gif"  width="450px" height="550px"/></kbd>
